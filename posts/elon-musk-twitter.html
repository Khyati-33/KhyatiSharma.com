Elon Musk's recent purchase of Twitter

by Siddharth Duggal on April 25th, 2022


For those that don't know yet, Elon Musk just bought Twitter for around $44 Billion USD. Why you may ask?A lot of people are still extremely confused on why he even bought the social media company. Personally, I think it's getting too much attention for what it is.

This recent purchase has made global news, on all media outlets. What even is twitter? It's just a place where people can inform people about things, right? Should it really get so much attention from the media?


Elon and Free speech

He claims he mainly bought twitter in intention to improve free speech on the platform. “I hope that even my worst critics remain on Twitter, because that is what free speech means.” Elon said in a tweet. When closing the deal with the previous owner of Twitter, he made sure that they agreed that he could make the company private.
